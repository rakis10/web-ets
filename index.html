  • Modernization of hydraulic infrastructure
  • Hydropower & waterworks asset management
  • Rehabilitation of dams & structures
  • Water-security & flood-protection projects

02Hydrometeorology

Slovak Hydrometeorological Institute

Top weather forecasts & warnings for Slovakia and Europe

Slovakia's national hydrometeorological authority under the Ministry of Environment — combining public service, scientific expertise and long-term data stewardship. A 24/7 operator of the European Flood Alert System (EFAS).

  • Flood forecasting & warning
  • Drought monitoring & climate risk
  • Hydrometeorological networks
  • Early warning for civil protection
  • Environmental & air-quality services
shmu.sk/en
